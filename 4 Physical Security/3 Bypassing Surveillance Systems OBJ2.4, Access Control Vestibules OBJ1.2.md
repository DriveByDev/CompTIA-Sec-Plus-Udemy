# Bypassing Surveillance Systems OBJ 2.4
# Bypass Surveillance Systems 
## Visual Obstruction 
- blocking the camera's line of sight (LoS), paint on the camera, etc 
## Blind Sensors & Cameras 
- overwhelming the sensor/camera with a sudden burst of light to render it ineffective for a limited period of time 
## Acoustic Interference 
- jamming/playing loud music to disrupt the microphone's functionality 
## Electromagnetic Interference (EMI)
- jamming the signals that surveillance systems rely on to monitor the env 
## Physical Env Attack
- exploiting the env around the surveillance equipment to compromise its functionality

# Access Control Vestibules OBJ 1.2
## Access Control Vestibules 
- designed with 2 doors that's electronically controlled to ensure only 1 door can be open at a given time
- provide a controlled env for anyone trying to access an orgs facilities 
## Piggybacking
- person with legitimate access intentionally allows another person w/o auth to enter a secure area with them 
## Tailgating 
- unauth person follows someone with legit access to the secure space w/o knowledge or consent
