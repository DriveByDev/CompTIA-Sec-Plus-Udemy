# Impersonation OBJ 2.2
- attack where an adversary assumes the identity of another person to gain unauth access to resources or steal sensitive data 
## Brand Impersonation
- specific form of impersonation where attacker pretends to represent legitimate company/brand 
## Typosquatting
- attacker registers domain name that's similar to popular website
- contains some kind of common typographical errors 
## Watering Hole Attacks 
- targeted form of cyber attack where attackers compromise specific website/service that their target is known to use

# Phishing Attacks OBJ 2.2
## Phishing 
- fraudulent attack using deceptive emails from trusted sources to trick individuals into disclosing personal info like passwords & credit card numbers 
## Spear Phishing
- tightly focused attackers on specific group of individuals or orgs 
- targeted methodology with higher success rate at deceiving recipients into believing emails are authentic 
## Whaling
- form of spear phishing targeting high-profile individuals, CEOs, CFOs
## Business Email Compromise (BEC)
- leverages internal email accounts within company to manipulate employees into carrying out malicious actions for the attacker 
## Vishing (Voice Phishing)
- phone-based attack in which attacker deceives victims into divulging personal or financial information 
## Smishing (SMS Phishing)
- attack that uses text messages to deceive individuals into sharing their personal information
